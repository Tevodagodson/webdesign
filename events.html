Annual Carolina Beach Music Festival

Your Pleasure Island Chamber of Commerce invites you and your family to join us on Saturday, June 3, 2023 for the 37th Annual Carolina Beach Music Festival that will draw beach music enthusiasts from across the East Coast to enjoy a day of great music, dancing, sunshine and some of the best eating establishments Carolina Beach and Pleasure Island have to offer.

Night with Kites

The Pleasure Island Chamber of Commerce kicked off this spectacular 3-day event with a "Night with Kites" on the beach in front of the Courtyard by Marriott, Carolina Beach! Spectators watched as our CFKF Flyers lit up the night sky and enjoyed hot cocoa, music and an incredible show! Then fun continued down to Fort Fisher State Recreation Area (1000 Loggerhead Road, Kure Beach) for two full days of kite festival fun on 11/5 & 11/6 from 10a-4pm!

Cape Fear Educational Series

The Education Series, a series of courses offered to Pleasure Island Chamber of Commerce members, offers a variety of 30 minute seminars with a 30 minute Q&A afterwards. Join us every month with some of the best, most knowledgeable in the New Hanover area.
